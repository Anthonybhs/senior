This is the Home of Recycle Bartlett High

Recycle Bartlett High is an initiative to reduce the paper foot print of Bartlett High school. Although Bartlett High has a 1:1 technology program many students and teachers still use paper, as a student who is in the honors and AP tracks I can attest that those students use a lot of paper. Sadly, some of this paper goes to the trash can due to the single purpose of it (i.e. Scantrons, interims, old double sided worksheets, etc)
